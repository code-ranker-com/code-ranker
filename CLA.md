## Corporate Contributor License Agreement (CCLA) — note

If You are making a Contribution on behalf of a company or other legal entity (e.g. as part
of Your employment), the entity should execute a **Corporate CLA** in addition to, or instead
of, the Individual CLA, designating the employees authorized to Submit Contributions. The
license grants are the same as Sections 2–3 above. Replace the "You" representations with
authorized-signatory representations on behalf of the entity.

*(If You need a separate CCLA document, one can be provided. For an early-stage project, the
Individual CLA above plus the employer-permission representation in Section 5.3 is usually
sufficient.)*
