# Contributing to code-ranker

Thanks for considering a contribution! Bug reports, docs fixes, and pull
requests are all welcome.

## Contributor License Agreement (CLA)

All contributions require agreeing to the project [CLA](./CLA.md). It is a
one-time step: on your first pull request the CLA-assistant bot will ask you
to sign, and after that all your future contributions are covered.

In short (the full text in [CLA.md](./CLA.md) is what governs): you keep the
copyright in your contribution, and you grant the project a broad license to
distribute it — including under other license terms in the future. Pull
requests cannot be merged until the CLA is signed.
